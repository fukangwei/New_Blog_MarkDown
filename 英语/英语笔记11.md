### vote

v，n；选票，投票，选举，推举<!--more-->

1. `The motion was passed by 6 votes to 3`(这项动议以6票对3票获得通过)
2. `take a vote on an issue`(对一问题进行表决)
3. `She obtained 40% of the vote`(她获得40%的选票)
4. `the student vote`(学生的投票总数)
5. `Did you vote for or against her?`(你投了她的赞成票还是反对票？)
6. `I vote (that) we go out to eat`(我提议我们到外面去吃饭)

### confusion

n；不确定，困惑，困窘，尴尬

1. `There is some confusion about what the correct procedure should be`(对于应该采取什么正确步骤，还是有些不明确)
2. `confusion between letters of the alphabet like 'o' or 'a'`(像o或a这类字母之间的混淆)
3. `He looked at me in confusion and did not answer the question`(他困窘地看着我，没有回答问题)
4. `Omissions in my recent article must have caused confusion`(我最近一篇文章中的几处省略肯定造成了困惑)
